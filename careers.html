Careers

Work that
compounds over time.

TAG is a small team that punches above its weight. We acquire and operate vertical software businesses — and we look for people who've done hard things and want to do them again, with more ownership, in a more interesting context.

We don't have a fund to return. We think in decades, not quarters. That changes the kind of work you do and the kind of person who thrives here.

Operator-first
Every person on the team has a real job. We don't carry overhead. If you're here, you're building something.
Build to keep
We buy businesses to run them — permanently. Your work has a longer half-life here than at a fund or a startup.
Global reach
Portfolio companies span 11+ countries. You'll work with operators across North America, Europe, Latin America, and beyond.
Move fast
Small team, short chains of command. Good ideas get implemented. Bad ones get killed quickly. No bureaucracy.

The Bench

Not every great operator
is between jobs right now.

TAG moves quickly after an acquisition. That means we need experienced leaders ready to step in on short notice — people who can stabilize an operation, earn the trust of an existing team, and start compounding value in the first 90 days.

We keep a bench of qualified operators across our verticals. When we acquire a business that needs new leadership, the bench is who we call first.

Have run a P&L, business unit, or full company
Comfortable stepping into an existing team and culture
Domain expertise in one or more of our verticals — or strong functional depth (finance, operations, sales)
Available to move when the right opportunity comes up
