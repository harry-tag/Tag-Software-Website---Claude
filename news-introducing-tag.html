Introducing TAG Software Group

We are proud to announce the launch of TAG Software Group, a newly formed part of Valsoft Corporation dedicated to acquiring and empowering exceptional vertical market software companies.

At TAG Software Group, we believe that long-term success in software is built on a foundation of trust, accountability, and entrepreneurial freedom. Our mission is to be a strategic partner by offering stability, resources and a clear vision — all while preserving the independence and culture that make each business unique.

Our Approach

With deep sector knowledge and a long-term investment model, TAG Software Group focuses on building sustainable growth. Unlike short-term investors, we buy, hold and support businesses for the future. We plan on holding and growing our portfolio companies indefinitely.

We operate a decentralised model that gives each portfolio company the space to continue leading with its own entrepreneurial agility, supported by our group's strategic expertise and resources. This ensures that your people remain empowered and your customers continue to receive the exceptional service they've come to expect.

All of this enables each portfolio company to grow without losing what made them successful.

A Valsoft Heritage

TAG Software Group was created as part of Valsoft Corporation's evolution. With more than 100 vertical market software companies acquired worldwide, Valsoft has demonstrated the power of long-term ownership and operational independence.

As Valsoft continues to grow, TAG Software Group is a continuation of that strategy. TAG carries forward Valsoft's proven model on a more focused scale, replicating the same commitment to sustainable growth, while building strong new partnerships across many industries.

Why Partner With Us

We offer:

  • A strong certainty of close — a transparent and reliable process.
  • A preservation of your legacy — safeguarding employees, culture, and reputation.
  • Long-term stewardship — investing in companies to grow steadily, not to flip.

Whether you are an advisor exploring options for your client, or a founder planning the next chapter of your business, TAG Software Group is ready to have that conversation.

Looking Ahead

Our website is now live, showcasing our portfolio, our team, and our philosophy. We invite you to explore and learn more about how we approach partnerships that last – and if this all sounds like a good fit for your business, get in touch to start the conversation.
