Investment criteria

What we look for.

A rough silhouette, not a hard filter. If you sit one or two dimensions off, talk to us anyway — almost none of our 28+ acquisitions were exactly on-spec.

Dimension Target What it really means
Revenue band $3M – $100M ARR We've closed deals well outside this band; it's the centre of gravity, not the wall.
Business model Recurring & mission-critical SaaS, on-prem with maintenance, transactional — anything customers can't realistically rip out.
Vertical B2B / B2G · single-vertical We prefer software where the buyer is a director of operations, not a CIO. Boring is good.
Customer count 100+ logos Concentration up to 25% on a single customer is fine if the relationship is decade-grade.
Geography North America · EU · UK · ANZ We operate cross-border; tax-resident jurisdiction is rarely a blocker.
Profitability EBITDA-positive preferred High-quality break-even is fine. We are not a turnaround shop, but we are patient.
Seller motivation Permanence > price If maximising headline price is the only goal, a strategic or PE buyer will beat us. We win on what comes after the wire.
